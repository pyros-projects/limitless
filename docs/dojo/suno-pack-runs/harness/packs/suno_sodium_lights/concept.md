# Sodium Lights

*Track concept — 2026-06-12 — suno-pack*

## Premise
The last hour of a night shift, when loneliness stops hurting and starts
glowing — a song about being the only one awake and almost liking it.

## Mood & Emotional Arc
Starts hushed and weary, lifts into warm resignation at the chorus,
peaks in a bridge that almost breaks into hope, ends settled — not
happy, but at peace with the hour.

## Imagery & Motifs
Sodium streetlights buzzing orange, a coffee going cold on the counter,
a bus that never comes, keys turning in a lock at dawn. Recurring motif:
light that hums.

## Sonic Palette
Late-70s midnight soul filtered through modern bedroom production. Warm
Rhodes chords, round muted bass, brushed drums, tape saturation, distant
vibraphone. Lead voice: tired, intimate male vocal, close-mic'd. ~72 BPM.

## Structure Sketch
Opens on Rhodes and room tone; verse 1 sparse; chorus brings bass and
brushed kit; verse 2 adds vibraphone; bridge strips to voice and bass
then swells; final chorus warmer, half-tempo outro fades on the hum.
